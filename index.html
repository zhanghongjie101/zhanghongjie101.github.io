Research areas

  • Cloud Computing
    Cloud computing is a type of distributed computing, which refers to the decomposition of huge data computing processing programs into countless small programs through the network “cloud”. Our research includes resource demand forecasting in the cloud, resource scheduling and allocation, network virtualization, etc.

  • Deep Reinforcement Learning
    Reinforcement learning (RL) is one of the paradigms and methodologies of machine learning. It is used to describe and solve the problem of agents’ maximizing returns or achieving specific goals through learning strategies during the interaction with the environment. Our research includes training acceleration, model compression, distributed training, etc.
